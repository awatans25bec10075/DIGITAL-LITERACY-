# Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic Report

For this project, I used Canva to design a one-page infographic on Digital Literacy Awareness. The infographic was created using a blue, white, and orange color theme to make it attractive and easy to read. I included three main topics: What is Digital Literacy, Safe Internet Practices, and Email Etiquette.

In the first section, I explained that digital literacy means using technology, websites, apps, and online communication in a smart and responsible way. In the second section, I added points about safe internet habits such as creating strong passwords, protecting personal information, and avoiding scams. In the third section, I explained good email etiquette, including using polite language, checking spelling, and writing clear subject lines.

I also used icons, illustrations, and different shapes to make the infographic more visually appealing. One interesting part of making this infographic was choosing the right visuals and arranging the content neatly so that the page looked full but not crowded.
