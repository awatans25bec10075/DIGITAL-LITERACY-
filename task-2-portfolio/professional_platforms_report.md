# Professional Platforms Report

I chose GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle as my professional platforms. GitHub is mainly used for storing projects, code, and technical work. I will use it to upload my programming projects, web development work, and engineering assignments. It will help me create a strong portfolio over the next four years.

LinkedIn is useful for building a professional network and sharing achievements. I will use it to connect with classmates, seniors, teachers, and industry professionals. I also plan to add my skills, certifications, internships, and projects to make my profile stronger.

Kaggle is a platform for learning data science, machine learning, and analytics. I will use it to practice Python, participate in competitions, and work on datasets. Since I am interested in AI and data analytics, Kaggle will help me improve my technical knowledge.

Over the next four years, these platforms will help me learn new skills, build my portfolio, and prepare for internships and future job opportunities.
